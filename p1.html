6 Features that every website should have

When people browse a website, they want easy navigation, attractive design, and relevant content. Not only these but there are other few requirements that would help you to build a catchy website.


Here I provide you the 6 most needed features that a website must have to make it easier for the user to access.

  • Easy navigation
  • Trendy, intuitive design and user experience
  • Relevant, authoritative website content
  • Product visuals and descriptions
  • Social media as an extension of the business website
  • Company blog

1. Easy Navigation Is the Most Useful Website Feature

A website that’s easy to navigate provides users with faster, more efficient access to content they want. People prefer a website with a clear menu structure that makes it easy to maneuver between pages.
Amazon is known for being easy to navigate, despite its high volume of content.



On its homepage, Amazon presents options for users to navigate and explore the site. By making menus easy to access and darkening the rest of the screen, people can focus on shopping categories and bypass the tempting offerings on the home screen.

Businesses should follow Amazon’s example and prioritize functionality and navigation for their users. Not doing so could mean losing potential customers.
